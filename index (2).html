✈️ Vacation week guide

A week off doesn't mean losing momentum — it means recharging so you come back stronger. Here's how to handle it.

📦 Before you go

Finish one full ML section before you leave so you feel good stepping away. Pre-schedule your Quran Hifz session for right after you return. Pack your acne cream, hair oil, and hair medicine — don't skip meds on vacation.

🌅 During the week

Keep prayers and Wird going — they take 20 min total and anchor the day. Do light bodyweight workouts (push-ups, squats) to maintain momentum, not full sessions, just movement. Allow yourself to fully rest, eat, explore, and enjoy it guilt-free. Take photos and notes if you want something to reflect on later.

💪 When you return

Don't try to "catch up" on everything at once. Just follow the schedule as if the next day after you left. One day back in routine is worth more than a stressed catch-up marathon.

📸 Make memories

Go out, explore, try local food, connect with people. This is part of a full life — not a break from it. A rested mind studies better, trains harder, and builds better projects.
